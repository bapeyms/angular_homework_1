Extreme Ironing: Pressing Limits

Extreme Ironing (EI) is an adrenaline-fueled outdoor activity that combines the thrills of an extreme sport with the satisfaction of a well-pressed shirt. It involves taking ironing boards to remote and dangerous locations — such as high mountain peaks, underwater, or even while skydiving — and ironing a few items of laundry. The official definition describes it as "the latest danger sport that combines the thrills of an extreme outdoor activity with the satisfaction of a well-pressed shirt." It is a perfect blend of British irony, physical challenge, and domestic duty.
